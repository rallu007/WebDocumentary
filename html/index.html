Social Responsability
If someone asked you to offer some hopeless
young people a chance for a future, what
would you do? Well, Metin tried to make a
difference for some of them.

He offered a job position to some boys
from different countries (for eg. Syria) for
two months and taught them how to work
and act properly. Now, they can have a
normal life where they can have a salary
and build a family.

This mindset - be always better - gave
Metin's "Best in town Award", and proved
once for all that passion, ambition and
interest in others can make you one of
the best.
